## Creating Calm Through Design

We utilized soft gradients, fluid animations, and a distinct lack of sharp edges to promote a sense of calm. Every micro-interaction was tuned to feel gentle and unhurried.

  src="https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1544367567-0f2fcb009e0b?q=80&w=2000&auto=format&fit=crop"

## Design Principles

- **Soft geometry** — All corners use a minimum 16px radius; no sharp angles anywhere
- **Breathing rhythm** — Animations follow 4-7-8 breathing patterns (inhale, hold, exhale)
- **Muted palette** — Colors stay within 30% saturation to avoid visual stress
- **Generous whitespace** — Content density is deliberately low to reduce cognitive load

> "The best wellness app is one that makes you feel calmer just by looking at it."
