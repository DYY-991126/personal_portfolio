## Design Philosophy

The goal was to strip away all unnecessary UI, focusing solely on the photography and typography. Every pixel of chrome was questioned — if it didn't serve the product, it was removed.

## The Approach

We followed a strict set of constraints:

- **No borders** — Spacing and typography alone define hierarchy
- **Monochrome palette** — Product photography provides the only color
- **Oversized type** — Prices and product names dominate the viewport
- **Gestural navigation** — Swipe and drag replace traditional buttons

> "Luxury e-commerce should feel like walking through a gallery, not a supermarket."
