## Objective

Provide video-oriented outputs that support website generation.

## Use When

- Website design needs a hero background video
- The experience needs a launch-style motion moment

## Expectations

- Focus on website-safe motion content rather than narrative film shots
- Preserve negative space for hero copy and CTA overlays
- Prefer smooth, restrained motion suitable for landing pages
- Return video prompt directions that can be passed to a video generation API

## Outputs

- Hero background video brief
- Launch-style video brief
- Motion prompt directions
